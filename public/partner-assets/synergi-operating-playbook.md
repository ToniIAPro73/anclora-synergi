# Partner Operating Playbook

## Objective

Provide a lightweight operating baseline for private collaboration between Anclora and approved Synergi partners.

## Referral Handling

1. Qualify the opportunity before introducing it into the ecosystem.
2. Share only the minimum information needed to activate the next step.
3. Keep decision owners and timing visible from the start.

## Communication Rules

- One owner per active opportunity
- Clear timestamps for pending actions
- Written confirmation after each relevant decision

## Asset Usage

- Use shared assets only inside the agreed collaboration perimeter.
- Do not redistribute private documents without explicit approval.
- Mark assets as reviewed once they have been processed operationally.

## Quality Bar

Every deliverable should reinforce:

- discretion
- premium service
- territorial intelligence
- disciplined follow-through
