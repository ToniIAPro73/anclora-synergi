# Synergi Onboarding Brief

## Purpose

This brief aligns the partner with the initial collaboration frame inside Anclora Synergi.

## Collaboration Principles

- Preserve the premium positioning of Anclora in every client-facing interaction.
- Prioritise traceability, response quality and curated handover notes.
- Escalate sensitive buyer or seller signals early.

## First 30 Days

1. Validate service scope, geography and preferred opportunity types.
2. Confirm core contacts, response channels and operating hours.
3. Review the first curated opportunities and the operating playbook.

## Delivery Standard

- Clear written updates
- Explicit next steps
- Premium tone
- No untracked referrals

## Workspace Expectation

Synergi will progressively publish private assets, curated opportunities and activity signals inside the partner workspace.
